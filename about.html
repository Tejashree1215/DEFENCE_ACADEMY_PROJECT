YUDH ABHYAS Academy is the brainchild of our officers (Ex- Indian Army).

The officers have min 22+ years of a distinguished career in uniform having held varied prestigious appointments during their service careers.

Since 2016-17, Our Officers have been closely involved in guiding students to achieve success at the SSB. As mentors, they have worked very closely (and continue to work) with students from many schools considered as “Feeder Institutes” for the prestigious National Defence Academy (NDA). They take pride in the fact that students mentored by them from such Institutes have seen huge success at the SSB and have successfully taken the first steps towards an Officers’ Commission in Indian Armed forces. Few of these are:

Sainik School – Satara

Sainik School – Chittorgarh

Services Preparatory Institute – Aurangabad

Army Institute Of Technology – Pune

Kolhapur Institute of Technology – Kolhapur
