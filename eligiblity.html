Eligiblity

Eligiblity for Indian Army

10+2/Intermediate exampassed in Science with Physics, Chemistry, Maths and English. Now eight age for higher qualification. 10+2/Intermediate exam passed in any stream(Arts,Commerce,Science)with 50% marks in aggregate and min40% in each subject. Weight age for higher qualification.

For more information visit https://indianarmy.nic.in

Eligiblity for Indian Navy

10+2 (B. Tech) 17 to 19 ½ 12th Pass with 70% in PCM and 50% in English in 10th or 12thstandard. Shortlisting of candidates for SSB will be based on JEE (Main) – All India Rank (AIR) for BE/B Tech curriculum.

For more information visit https://www.joinindiannavy.gov.in

Eligiblity for Indian Air Force

IAF provides opportunity for airmen with minimum 10+2 educational qualification and below the age of 27 years to become commissioned officer in Army through Army Cadet College Course. The eligibility candidate has to pass the written examination conducted by Army HQ. On passing the exam they were interviewed by SSB.

For more information visit https://indianairforce.nic.in
