We Do Not Coach... We "Guide"

The uniqueness of YUDH ABHYAS Academy is its specialized and result oriented approach to SSB guidance and written exam. We are conscious of the big difference between "coaching" and "guidance". Since the SSB aims at evaluating personality, our efforts are always towards guiding candidates to understand themselves better and give their best. Our experience has shown us that coaching students to respond / answer in a particular way (as practised in certain institutes) actually causes more harm and prevents candidates from giving their best. However, guidance helps them to be natural while being at their best.

We Follow A Systematic, Result-Oriented Approach

All sessions at YUDH ABHYAS Academy follow a very systematic approach, which includes complete familiarisation with the “two-stage” SSB Selection System and detailed discussions about the correct approach to the tests, followed by personalised evaluation and feedback.

We Have Highly Qualified & Experienced Assessors

We have DIPR trained and qualified assessors who have served as SSB panel members. They have over 22+ years of uniformed service and over 12+ years of combined SSB panel experience. During their tenure at the SSBs they have been involved in assessing thousands of students. Many candidates selected by them are proud commissioned officers today.

We Have Excellent Infrastructure

Our guidance centre is equipped with all the necessary infrastructure needed to impart guidance for the SSB, giving the candidates a real feel of the entire process. We have a well-equipped classroom for conducting all tests and discussion sessions. Further, our guidance centre is one of the few institutes that has full size GTO tasks for PGT / HGT / etc. Our GTO Area helps the candidates to get a real time experience of group discussions and such other group activities, almost akin to what they would face at the SSB.
